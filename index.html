Who I Am

I am Sofonias Teshome, a dedicated real estate sales agent at Metro Real Estate™ — one of Ethiopia's most distinguished and trusted property companies. My mission is simple: to connect people with spaces they are proud to call home.

Based in Addis Ababa, I specialize in helping clients navigate Ethiopia's dynamic real estate market. Whether you are a first-time buyer, an investor seeking opportunity, or a family ready to upgrade — I bring expertise, honesty, and genuine care to every transaction.
